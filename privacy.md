# 337 Time Tracker — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 6 May 2026_

337 Time Tracker is a local-first macOS application. We designed it so your data never leaves your Mac. This policy explains exactly what that means.

## What we collect

**Nothing about you, your work, or your time entries.**

All time tracking data — projects, tasks, notes, timestamps, goals, habits — is stored exclusively on your Mac in a local SQLite database. It is never transmitted to us or to any third party.

## What Apple collects

When you purchase a subscription or lifetime license, Apple processes the payment. We receive only:

- An anonymous transaction identifier
- - Your subscription status (active, expired, cancelled)
  - - Your country of purchase (for tax purposes)
   
    - We never receive your name, email, payment method, or Apple ID.
   
    - ## Crash reports
   
    - If you have macOS crash reporting enabled in System Settings, Apple may share anonymised crash logs with us. These contain no personal information or app data. You can disable this in: **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements**.
   
    - ## Analytics
   
    - We use no third-party analytics services. No telemetry. No tracking. No fingerprinting.
   
    - ## iCloud sync (future feature)
   
    - If you opt into iCloud sync in a future version, your data is encrypted and stored in your private iCloud account. We never have access to it.
   
    - ## Children
   
    - 337 Time Tracker is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, regardless of age.
   
    - ## Changes to this policy
   
    - If we ever change this policy, we'll update the date at the top and post a notice in the app. Material changes only apply to future versions.
